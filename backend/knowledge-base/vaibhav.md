## Work — Vaaree (Jun 2024 – Mar 2025)

Vaaree is a pre-Series A e-commerce startup in the home décor space. I joined as Product Manager for the website and app. The team was small and scrappy. My mandate was to turn the app from a quiet, underused channel into the primary growth driver.

I led a five-person product-engineering crew and took over as interim tech lead for nine months when the engineering leadership was in flux. I rebuilt the navigation and discovery flows around what users were actually trying to do (find and buy beautiful objects for their homes) rather than what the original architecture assumed.

Results: MAU grew from 1,000 to 80,000 in nine months. CTR and conversions on key flows doubled. Sprint completion rate went from 20% to 70% during my time as interim tech lead.

The home décor category taught me a lot about visual merchandising translated into digital UX — how colour, texture, and room context drive purchase intent differently from most other e-commerce categories.


## Work — Supertails (Nov 2021 – Mar 2024)

Supertails is a pet-care e-commerce platform. I joined as a founding-team Product Manager at Seed stage and stayed through Series B. The company grew to ₹200Cr ARR during that period.

This was my most formative PM role. I owned the app from scratch — there was no app when I joined. I built the product roadmap, ran user research, and shipped everything from core catalogue flows to recommendation systems to a mini in-app game I coded myself in vanilla JavaScript because the engineering team was stretched and the data clearly showed that engagement was the unlock we needed.

Key wins:
- The app reached 1M+ downloads and became the #1 rated app in the pet-care category on both iOS and Android.
- A search and discovery overhaul I led drove a 2× improvement in search-to-purchase conversion, contributing ~₹60Cr in incremental ARR.
- A personalised recommendation engine I built added +3% to average order value, contributing ~₹10Cr ARR.
- An Add-to-Cart flow redesign drove +30% improvement and ~₹50Cr ARR.
- The in-app mini-game I shipped single-handedly increased in-app session time by 12%.

The founding-team experience meant wearing many hats — I handled customer interviews, data analysis, stakeholder management, and occasionally wrote production code when the team needed it.


## Work — HolidayIQ (Dec 2017 – Oct 2019)

HolidayIQ was India's largest online travel community at the time. I joined as a Software Engineer straight out of BITS Pilani. This was my first real job and where I learned what "production" actually means.

My main project was rebuilding the lead-assignment engine — the system that matched travel intent signals to travel agents, which was the core of the platform's monetisation. I rebuilt it from scratch and maintained 100% uptime through the migration, which was the most technically demanding thing I'd done at that point.

I also built a vendor selection feature that let users choose their travel agent from a shortlist, and shipped call masking so that phone numbers between users and agents stayed private.

The role taught me engineering discipline, and being close to the business gave me an early hunger to move into product. The SDE-to-PM transition happened a couple of years later when I joined Supertails.
